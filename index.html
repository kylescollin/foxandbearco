Who We Are

Fox & Bear Co. is a collaborative effort to share with you our favorite treats and creations. Born and bred in Northern California, our Brooklyn kitchen is a haven to experiment, and perfect what we know. Taking inspiration from our friends, our city, and the great outdoors, we share our products with utmost pride.
