Our Story

Fox & Bear Co. was founded with a thirst to create, and the spirit to explore. We wanted to develop a beautiful and functional website in order to share our creations with friends and family. Our entrepreneurial spirit invited collaboration and well as trials and errors. With an open mind, and hungry tummy, our goodies are tried and true to the last bite. We have enjoyed making every piece of this homemade business. From pickles to skincare to paper goods, we will continue to invent and discover. Please use our contact page to send us a message of motivation, or a message for improvement.

Our Beliefs

We love food, neat things, and especially making them ourselves. All our products are made to order and preservatives are never added. Every jar, loaf or piece you receive is made with our favorite ingredients or created by our tried and true methods. Finally, they are sealed and labeled with love.
